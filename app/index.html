Is it a Headache or a Migraine Headache?

A migraine is more than just a headache. You may also have pain from light, sound, or fragrance, or may get sick to your stomach. These problems often come with migraine headaches.

More specifically, The International Headache Society describes a migraine as a headache that may:

  • Last between 4 and 72 hours
  • Has at lease two of the following symptoms:
    • Is focused on one side of the head
    • Pulses
    • Has moderate to severe pain
    • Is worse when you move
